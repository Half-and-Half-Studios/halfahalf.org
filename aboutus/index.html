ABOUT US

Our Story

Half & Half Studios is a game development studio founded in 2023 by Seh and AxoTheDev, formerly known as Sehelinda and Axoloch. The studio was originally created for Roblox, where we developed many game concepts, some of which were only ideas, while others were officially published.

Our Community

Over time, Half & Half Studios built a small but meaningful community, reaching 44 users and 9 staff members. We also created a small Discord community, with the goal of welcoming more members and growing alongside our players.

Moving Forward

After 2.5 years of making games on Roblox, we decided in 2026 to private our previous projects and begin creating games outside of the platform. This decision was made because of the Roblox release fees and the limitations we faced while developing there.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to create games that can be exciting and enjoyable for every kind of player. We also listen to ideas, requests, and wishes from our community, so we can make our games as fun and polished as possible.
