About

Who we are

Half & Half Studios started small, stayed small on purpose, and is still figuring out exactly what it wants to be.

  1. Our story

    2023 — Founded

    Half & Half Studios is a game development studio founded in 2023 by Seh and AxoTheDev, formerly known as Sehelinda and Axoloch. The studio was originally built for Roblox, where we developed many game concepts — some that stayed ideas, others that made it to release.

  2. Our community

    Ongoing

    Over time, we built a small but meaningful community — 44 users and 9 staff members strong. We also run a small Discord server, with the goal of welcoming more members and growing alongside our players.

  3. Moving forward

    2026 — Pivot

    After 2.5 years of making games on Roblox, we decided in 2026 to make our previous projects private and start building outside the platform — a call made because of Roblox's release fees and the limitations we kept running into.

Where we're headed

Our goal

Our main goal is to create games that are exciting and enjoyable for every kind of player. We listen to ideas, requests, and wishes from our community, so we can make what we build as fun and polished as possible.
